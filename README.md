I use motor behaviour as a rigorous window into general principles of prediction, inference, and learning — combining human experiments, computational modelling, EMG, and neural recordings. My published work spans *PNAS*, *PLOS Biology*, and the *Journal of Neurophysiology*.

My open-source toolboxes form a single research pipeline:

> **collect** behaviour → **model** learning → **measure** physiology &amp; movement quality → **decode** neural activity → **model** neural dynamics

### 🧪 Behaviour &amp; learning
- **[reach-task](https://github.com/BabaAdarsh/reach-task)** — dependency-free browser framework for sensorimotor adaptation experiments (visuomotor rotation, implicit error-clamp, target jump, mirror reversal). Runs free on GitHub Pages. *JavaScript · 22 tests.* · **[live demo](https://babaadarsh.github.io/reach-task/)**
- **[motor-adaptation-models](https://github.com/BabaAdarsh/motor-adaptation-models)** — canonical state-space models of motor learning (single/dual-rate, memory-of-errors) with fitting &amp; AIC model comparison. *Python · 12 tests.*

### 💪 Physiology &amp; movement quality
- **[emg-pipeline](https://github.com/BabaAdarsh/emg-pipeline)** — surface-EMG processing: filtering, envelopes, co-contraction indices, NMF muscle synergies. *Python · 16 tests.*
- **[kinematics-features](https://github.com/BabaAdarsh/kinematics-features)** — interpretable movement biomarkers: SPARC smoothness, submovements, variability. *Python · 12 tests.*

### 🧠 Neural decoding &amp; dynamics
- **[neural-decoding-kit](https://github.com/BabaAdarsh/neural-decoding-kit)** — decode movement from cortical populations: population-vector, Wiener, and Kalman (BCI) decoders, from scratch in NumPy. *Python · 6 tests.*
- **[task-trained-rnn](https://github.com/BabaAdarsh/task-trained-rnn)** — an RNN trained (from-scratch BPTT) to reach develops the rotational population dynamics of motor cortex (Churchland 2012); includes jPCA. *Python · 9 tests.*

### 🦾 Embodied control (research program)
- **[motornet-experiments](https://github.com/BabaAdarsh/motornet-experiments)** — three novel pre-registered experiments in [MotorNet](https://elifesciences.org/articles/88591): emergent impedance under uncertainty, reliability-weighted multisensory feedback, and contextual competing memories — with a tested analysis layer. *Python · 13 tests.*
